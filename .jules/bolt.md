## 2025-05-24 - Zustand Destructuring Re-renders | **Learning:** Destructuring `useGameStore()` in complex components (like Tabs) causes them to subscribe to the *entire* state object. This means any state change (like rapidly updating particles or xp) triggers full re-renders of the component, destroying performance. | **Action:** Always use targeted selectors `useGameStore(state => state.prop)` or `useShallow` for multiple properties to isolate re-renders.
