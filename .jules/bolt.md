## 2024-05-08 - Zustand Selector Optimization | **Learning:** Destructuring `useGameStore()` without specific selectors or `useShallow` subscribes the component to the entire global state, causing massive unnecessary re-renders across all tabs, especially since the `particles` state updates constantly (passive earnings). | **Action:** Always use specific state selectors (e.g., `useGameStore(state => state.property)`) or `useShallow` from `zustand/react/shallow` when multiple properties are needed.
